DraftBears Sportsbook

A data analysis project on NBA player performance metrics.

This project was created by Allen Dufort, Melvin He, and José Guallpa as a final project for the course CS 1951A: Data Science at Brown University in Spring 2024. For DraftBears Sportsbook, our goal was to improve sports analytics and sports predictions for sports fans by exploring the dynamics of NBA player performances. We focused on analyzing player statistics across different times of the NBA season to understand the correlations between various player performance metrics and their impact on the team's performance.
